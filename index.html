About Our School

A new school, built on old-fashioned values

Maa Kaushalaya Public School is located in Tharbitia and offers a structured, discipline-led education from Nursery through the middle grades. Our approach combines a strong academic foundation with attention to each child's discipline, character and co-curricular growth.

We follow a CBSE-aligned curriculum delivered through smart classrooms, regular assessment, and a low student-teacher ratio that lets every child be known by name.
